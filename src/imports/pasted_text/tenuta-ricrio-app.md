Design and prototype a premium SaaS-style web application called **“Tenuta Ricrio”** for automated rental contract generation and management for luxury villas in Tuscany.

The application is an internal management platform used by the staff of Tenuta Ricrio to generate multilingual rental contracts automatically for villa bookings.

The prototype must be highly realistic, modern, elegant, interactive, and visually polished — similar to a real production-ready startup SaaS product.

# Core User Flow

The prototype should demonstrate the complete workflow:

1. Staff logs into the platform
2. Creates a new contract
3. Selects a villa
4. Selects contract language
5. Inserts customer data
6. Inserts booking details
7. Generates automatic contract preview
8. Downloads or sends contract
9. Tracks contract status
